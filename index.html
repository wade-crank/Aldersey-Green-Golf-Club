Our Course

Set in 200 acres of Cheshire countryside, Aldersey Green Golf Club has a golf course that is envied by many but rivalled by few in terms of sheer enjoyment to play. Many of the fairways are tree-lined and narrow making accuracy from the tee absolutely pivotal to scoring well. The course is also home to 14 lakes & ponds, so by warned that any stray shots can, and on many occasions will be punished. Packing a few extra balls in your bag might be a good idea!
