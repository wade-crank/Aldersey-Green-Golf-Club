Team Member

  • Name: Jason Davies
  • Title: Coaching Professional
  • Bio: Jason is our PGA Coaching Professional. He has been recognised as one of the UK's Top 100 PGA Professionals and am the England Golf coach for the West Midlands Under 18s squad. Jason grew up on the Wirral playing amateur golf as a member of Heswall Golf Club before beginning a golfing career at Upton-by-Chester Golf Club. Since turning professional in 1994, He has worked across Europe, first at a Gary Player Academy and then at Golf & Country Club Markgraeflerland in Germany. Jason is also the England Golf Under 18s West Mids squad coach and, since 2009, he has been the Lead Coach for Shropshire and Herefordshire County Golf Partnership. Jason has competed as a player on both the Alps and Challenge tours and, through his ranking in Switzerland, represented the PGA in the International Team Championship and European PGA Championship.
